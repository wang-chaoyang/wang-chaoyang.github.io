<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

I am a PhD Student at School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Peking University (PKU), advised by Prof. [Yunhai Tong](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=T4gqdPkAAAAJ), and a research intern at Baidu, working with [Jingdong Wang](https://jingdongwang2017.github.io/) and [Haifeng Wang](https://haifengwang.net/).

My research interests include visual generative models and multimodal large language models (MLLMs).
